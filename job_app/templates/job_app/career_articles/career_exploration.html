Career Exploration



Exploring different career paths can help you discover new opportunities and find a career that aligns with your interests and skills. Here are some ways to explore different career paths:

  • Job shadowing: Job shadowing involves spending a day or more with someone who works in a career that interests you. This can provide valuable insights into what the job is really like.
  • Internships: Internships allow you to gain hands-on experience in a particular field while also building your resume and networking with professionals.
  • Informational interviews: Informational interviews involve speaking with professionals in a career field to learn more about their experiences and the requirements
  • Research: Use online resources to research different career paths and industries. This can help you gain a better understanding of the skills, education, and experience required for each career.
  • Volunteer: Volunteering can provide opportunities to gain experience in a particular field while also giving back to your community.
